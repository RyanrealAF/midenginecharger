Drivetrain & Power Delivery

The drivetrain components are selected and specified to handle peak torque loads while ensuring reliable and efficient power transfer to the wheels. Focus is placed on durability and shift speed.

Transmission and Clutch

A sequential six-speed gearbox is employed for rapid gear changes. The unit is rated for 1200 Nm of torque. A triple-plate carbon clutch is paired with it for high thermal capacity and engagement consistency.

Differential and Final Drive

A mechanical limited-slip differential (LSD) with a fixed 4.10 final drive ratio is used. The LSD features a 60/40 ramp angle split for balanced acceleration lockup and deceleration control.
